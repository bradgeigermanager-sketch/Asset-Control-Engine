### Keeping the Checklist Live Through Development
- **Version the checklist** in the product backlog (e.g., `checklist:v1.0`) and update as features are implemented or risks change.  
- **Sprint cadence updates**: add a checklist review item to each sprint retrospective until GA.  
- **Automated verification**: tie acceptance criteria to CI tests and integration smoke tests so checklist items flip to “done” automatically when tests pass.  
- **Quarterly risk review**: Security and Compliance owners revalidate high‑risk items (RBAC, audit trail, sanitization).  
- **Change log and approvals**: every checklist change requires a documented owner and approval (product + security).
