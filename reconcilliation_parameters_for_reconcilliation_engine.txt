### Summary
Below are **production‑ready reconciliation parameters** you can drop into the reconciliation engine. They include **field thresholds**, **scoring weights**, **auto‑link policies by asset class**, **security controls**, and **operational limits**. I provide both **JSON** and **YAML** formats plus a short application and tuning procedure.

### How to apply these parameters
- **Load file** into the reconciliation service configuration store (use the `version` field for safe rollbacks).  
- **Start in suggest mode**: set `autoLink` to `false` globally for the first 48 hours while monitoring metrics.  
- **Enable per‑class autoLink** gradually: enable `laptop` then `vm` then `server` after each 24–48 hour validation window.  
- **Enforce security controls** before enabling auto‑create: require `mfaRequiredFor.createAssetFromDiscovery` and `dualApprovalFor.redeployAsset`.  
- **Wire metrics** to your monitoring system and create alerts for `coverageBelow` and `falsePositiveAbove`.

---

### Tuning procedure
1. **Baseline run**: run seeded dataset and collect metrics.  
2. **Conservative start**: keep `autoLink` off; review suggested matches for 48 hours.  
3. **Adjust thresholds**: if coverage < target, lower `fuzzyMatch.compositeScoring.autoLinkScoreThreshold` in 0.02 increments; re-run tests.  
4. **Monitor FP**: if false positives rise, raise hostname/model thresholds or increase weight of `serial` and `mac`.  
5. **Field tuning**: adjust `hostname.thresholds` by length bucket if many short‑name FPs occur.  
6. **Operationalize rollback**: ensure `autoLinkRollbackWindowHours` and rollback procedure are tested.  
7. **Weekly review**: review metrics, manual queue backlog, and adjust `operationalLimits` and SLA values.

---

### Acceptance metrics and monitoring
- **Coverage target**: **≥ 0.95** (95%) of expected matches.  
- **False positive ceiling**: **< 0.02** (2%).  
- **Conflict rate alert**: > 10% triggers investigation.  
- **Manual queue SLA**: average resolution time ≤ **24 hours**.  
- **AutoLink rollback tested**: rollback procedure validated in staging.
